# Credit Card Weekly Financial & Customer Performance Dashboard

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a **comprehensive weekly credit card dashboard** that provides real-time visibility into **financial performance, customer behavior, delinquency patterns, and operational efficiency**.  
The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor trends, evaluate risk, and make data-driven decisions across credit card operations.

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Weekly Revenue Trend (WoW Analysis)
- Revenue shows **significant week-over-week fluctuations**, emphasizing the importance of weekly monitoring.
- **Week 53 recorded the highest revenue**, with a **28.8% WoW increase**, driven by higher transaction activity.
- Several weeks experienced revenue decline, highlighting cyclical spending patterns.

**Insight:**  
Short-term revenue volatility makes weekly analysis critical for proactive business decisions.

---

### 2Ô∏è‚É£ Delinquent Account Analysis
- Only **6.06% of accounts are delinquent**, while **93.94% remain non-delinquent**.
- Delinquency is marginally higher among **female customers**.
- **Self-employed and businessman segments** contribute relatively more to delinquency compared to salaried groups.

**Insight:**  
Delinquency risk is concentrated within specific occupation segments rather than the overall customer base.

---

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Card Activation within 30 Days
- **57.46% of customers activate their card within 30 days**, indicating strong onboarding effectiveness.
- Activation rates are slightly higher among **female customers**.
- **Medium and low-income groups** show higher activation compared to high-income customers.

**Insight:**  
Onboarding strategies are effective overall, but high-income customers may need tailored engagement approaches.

---

### 4Ô∏è‚É£ Personal Loan vs Delinquency Behavior
- **87.27% of customers do not hold personal loans**.
- Customers with personal loans show a **higher likelihood of delinquency**, though overall impact is limited due to low delinquent share.

**Insight:**  
Personal loan cross-selling should be accompanied by enhanced credit risk monitoring.

---

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ Revenue Contribution by Card Category
- **Blue Card holders contribute ~83% of total revenue**, driven by a large customer base.
- **Silver and Gold cards** show higher customer satisfaction scores despite lower volumes.
- **Platinum cards** represent a premium segment with limited customers.

**Insight:**  
Mass-market cards drive revenue volume, while premium cards drive customer experience.
